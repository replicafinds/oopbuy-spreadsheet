If you have never used OopBuy before, this guide covers everything from registration to receiving your first haul. The process is straightforward once you understand how purchasing agents work.

What OopBuy Is

OopBuy is a purchasing agent — they act as a middleman between you and Chinese domestic sellers on platforms like Weidian and Taobao. You give OopBuy a product link, they buy it, warehouse it, take QC photos, and ship it to you internationally when you approve.

The key insight is that you are not buying from OopBuy's own inventory. You are using their buying and forwarding service to access sellers who only operate within the Chinese domestic market.

Step 1 — Register

Create an account at OopBuy. Use invite code G6N5UV92Q during registration for member pricing from your first order. The registration process is straightforward and requires only an email address.

Step 2 — Find Products

Do not search OopBuy directly. The search results are populated with thousands of listings with no quality differentiation. Instead, use the OopBuy Spreadsheet at replicafind.com/oopbuy-spreadsheet. The spreadsheet provides direct links to specific seller pages that the community has verified.

Copy the seller link from the spreadsheet, paste it into OopBuy's product search, and the specific item will appear with pricing.

Step 3 — Add to Cart and Submit Order

Once you have the specific product page, select your size and color, then submit the order through OopBuy. OopBuy will purchase the item from the seller and send you a confirmation.

Step 4 — QC Photos

When the item arrives at OopBuy's warehouse, they will take QC photos and upload them to your account. Review every photo carefully before approving. For shoes, check sole unit, toe box, and logos. For clothing, check fabric, embroidery or badges, and stitching.

If anything looks wrong, you can reject the item and OopBuy will contact the seller for a replacement or refund.

Step 5 — Consolidate and Ship

If you have multiple items, consolidate them into a single package before shipping. OopBuy charges per package, not per item. Shipping 6 items consolidated costs roughly the same as shipping 2 individually.

Once you approve consolidation, choose a shipping route, pay the shipping fee, and your haul is on its way.
